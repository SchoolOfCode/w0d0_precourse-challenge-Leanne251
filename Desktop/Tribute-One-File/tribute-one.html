What are Essential Oils?

Essential Oils are concentrated extracts from plants which many people believe to have positive impacts on health & wellbeing. They can be used for a number of things, including:

  • Aromatherpy
  • Diffuser Oils & Home Fragrances
  • Massage Oils
  • Combating Illness
  • Skincare

This page focuses on Essential Oils that can be used in your daily skincare regime.

How Essential Oils Can Be Used In Skincare

It is important that essential oils are combined with a carrier oil. The potency of essential oil can cause skin irritation if not diluted and a little goes a long way.
To dilute your essential oils to be used with skin care, combine them with a carrier oil such as sweet almond oil, jojoba oil or Coconut Oil. All of which have great skincare benefits themselves, adding moisture and nurishment when applied. You can also add cocoa butter to make a cream & try whipping with your electric whisk for a really luxurious finish!

Recipe

  • 25g unrefined cocoa butter
    • To hydrate & nourish skin and improve elasticity
  • 25g unrefined shea butter
    • To protect against skin damage from pollutants
  • 50ml sweet almond oil
    • Can improve texture and skin tone
  • 10g arrow root powder
    • To remove the greasy feel caused by the oil
  • 10ml of Vitamin E oil
    • Effective at reducing UV damage in skin
  • 3 drops of Lavender essential oil
    • Improves blood circulation and promotes a youthful complexion.
  • 3 drops of Tea Tree essential oil
    • Keeps pores clear because of its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties
